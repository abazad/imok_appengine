Download IMOk for Blackberry Bold
  1. On your BlackBerry, open a web browser and point it to:

    http://imokapp.appengine.com/bb

  2. Click "Download."

  3. You'll find the app in the "Downloads" folder. Click on the "IMOk" icon to start.

  4. Click "Yes" to grant IMOk Trusted Application status. This lets IMOk send SMS messages and you'll only see this prompt once.

  5. Start the app again and you're ready to go!
